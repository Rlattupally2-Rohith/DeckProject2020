Step 1:- The Base

Part A: Mixing Cement

First thing we did was ensure we had a stable base. So we dug 6ft holes that needed to be filled with cement We had to mix dry cement with water and then mix it thoroughly. We used a hand mixer and we filled all the holes and we finished the first step in a day.
